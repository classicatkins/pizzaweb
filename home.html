About Us

At Pizza Parlor, we know that the secret to great pizza is in the ingredients. That's why we only use the freshest and highest quality ingredients to create our mouth-watering pizzas. From our vine-ripened tomatoes to our handcrafted dough, every element of our pizzas is carefully selected to deliver the best possible flavor and texture. Our expert chefs take pride in their craft, and work tirelessly to ensure that each pizza is cooked to perfection, with just the right balance of toppings, sauce, and crust.

But pizza isn't the only thing we're good at. We also offer a wide selection of sides to complement your meal, from garlic bread to mozzarella sticks and wings. Whether you're looking for a hearty appetizer or a tasty accompaniment to your pizza, we've got you covered. And with our friendly staff, comfortable atmosphere, and unbeatable prices, Pizza Parlor is the perfect destination for a night out with friends or a family dinner. So come on in, and experience the difference that fresh ingredients and expert craftsmanship can make!

At Pizza Parlor, we understand that everyone has different dietary needs and preferences. That's why we offer a range of options to accommodate a variety of dietary needs, including gluten-free and vegetarian options. Our gluten-free crust is made with high-quality ingredients and is available in all sizes, so you can enjoy your favorite toppings on a delicious, gluten-free pizza. And for our vegetarian customers, we offer a variety of vegetable toppings, as well as meatless options like our Margherita pizza and Veggie Lovers pizza.

In addition to our commitment to quality ingredients and dietary accommodation, we also prioritize exceptional customer service. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always available to answer any questions you may have and ensure that your dining experience is enjoyable from start to finish. We also offer convenient online ordering for those on-the-go or looking for a contactless option.

So whether you're looking for a classic pepperoni pizza or a gluten-free vegetarian option, Pizza Parlor has got you covered. Come in and experience our commitment to quality ingredients, expert craftsmanship, and exceptional customer service today!
